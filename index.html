Currently, I focus on deep learning solutions to biometric identifcation problems at Element. Previously, I was a Research Scientist at Uber AI, working with Jeff Clune on applied reinforcement learning and architecture search. Before that, I was a graduate student the Berkeley Artificial Intelligence Research (BAIR) Lab, advised by Trevor Darrell. There, I worked on perception systems for autonomous driving with Berkeley Deep Drive. An overview of my research can be found here.


My interests lie at the intersection of machine learning, computer vision, and reinforcment learning. I am particularly interested in the applications of ML to autonomous driving, healthcare, and education. Specifically, I'm interested in the wider adoption of RL solutions to these problems.
